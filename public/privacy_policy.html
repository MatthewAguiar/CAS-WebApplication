SCPSCAS Official Privacy Policy

What is SCPSCAS: SCPSCAS is a small website being created as a school project for the Sturgis Charter Public School of Cape Cod Massachusetts. The website’s intent is to interact with user’s Google Apps such as Gmail and Calendar. Users will be able to use those two apps in this website to organize their community service hours (CAS hours) in an efficient manner.

What information does this website collect: This website collects the user's email address for Google Gmail and their calendars for usage with Google Calendar.

How does this website use the information: This website uses the user’s email to send emails to community service advisors and uses their Google Calendar App to create and reorganize their community service events.

How will SCPSCAS use your Google Data: SCPSCAS will access your Google Calendar and Email to create a new Google Calendar that manages your CAS experiences and send emails that to supervisors for confirmation of your experience’s success(or failure).
